## How these skills are usually applied

- Building decision-support dashboards for managers and operational teams.
- Automating repetitive internal workflows to save time and reduce errors.
- Supporting learners and organizations that want a clearer introduction to data science and AI.
- Connecting research thinking with practical implementation in business and community settings.
