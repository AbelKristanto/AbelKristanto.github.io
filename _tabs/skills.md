## <span class="lang-en">How I usually work</span><span class="lang-id">Cara saya biasanya bekerja</span>

<div class="section-card-grid">
  <div class="section-card">
    <h3><span class="lang-en">1. Understand the goal</span><span class="lang-id">1. Memahami tujuan</span></h3>

  </div>

  </div>
  <div class="section-card">
    <h3><span class="lang-en">3. Make the result understandable</span><span class="lang-id">3. Membuat hasil mudah dipahami</span></h3>
    <p><span class="lang-en">The final result should help people act, not just admire the technique.</span><span class="lang-id">Hasil akhir harus membantu orang bertindak, bukan hanya mengagumi tekniknya.</span></p>
